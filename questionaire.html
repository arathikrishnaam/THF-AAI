Autism Awareness Assessment

1. Have you ever heard the term "autism"?

2. Do you know what autism is?

3. Is autism curable?

4. Do you know someone with autism?

5. If you knew someone with autism, would you invite them to your birthday party or event?

6. Do you believe people with autism can live independently and succeed in life?

7. What do you think is the best way to support someone with autism?

8. Do you believe schools and workplaces are inclusive enough for people with autism?

9. Have you ever tried to learn more about autism (e.g., reading, attending events)?

10. Would you like to know more about how to support and interact with people on the autism spectrum?
